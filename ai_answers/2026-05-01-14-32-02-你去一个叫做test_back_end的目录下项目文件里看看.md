| 项目路径 | 虚拟环境 |
|---------|---------|
| `/root/test_back_end/stock_data_service/` ✅ | `/root/test_back_end/venv/` |
| `/root/django/stock_data_service/` ✅ | `/root/django/stock_data_service/venv/` |
